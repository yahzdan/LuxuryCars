Live to Stand Out. BRABUS 600 PEETCH.

The BRABUS 600 PEETCH brings the striking BRABUS “Peetch” aesthetic to a different kind of powerhouse. Based on the Range Rover P530, this 600-horsepower Masterpiece SUV combines a luxurious off-road heritage with a design that is anything but ordinary. It is a bold expression of luxury, as unique as it is striking. Inside and out, it is a style-forward supercar with a powerful feel-good factor, giving undeniable proof to the idea that standout style doesn’t have to compromise performance.

Interior. Peetch.

Get behind the wheel and immerse yourself in a style-forward BRABUS Masterpiece interior design that exudes maximum individuality in every single detail. Matching the exterior style, the BRABUS 600 PEETCH’s entire cabin is adorned in the same exclusive "Peetch" colorway, from the seat surfaces to the door panels and even the flooring. The luxurious leather upholstery features intricate BRABUS Seashell Diamond quilting, adding a unique touch of BRABUS typical sophistication. Aluminum pedals and carbon fiber elements integrated throughout the cockpit—including the dashboard trims, door panels and middle consoles—introduce a classic high-performance edge, contrasting with the extravagantly styled leatherwork.
