ALL GRAY EVERYTHING. BRABUS 1000 ALL GRAY.

The BRABUS 1000 "ALL GRAY" is a unique transfer of the design ethos and power of the BRABUS ROCKET 1000 to a new platform.
Based on the Mercedes-AMG S 63 E Performance, this unapologetic Masterpiece supercar of the BRABUS Collection 2025 in-
herits the absolute maximum of what we have to offer. It was designed to captivate, impressing with a unique, monochrome
gray signature look and harnessing every cubic inch of its next-level performance figures in the creation of an undeniable 1-
Second-Wow effect.

Interior. Masterpiece.

Naturally, a fully specced BRABUS supercar also includes a tailor-made Masterpiece interior, designed and made by hand at the BRABUS upholstery shop. Matching the exterior color, the entire interior from the seat surfaces to the headliner features especially soft and breathable gray leather. Even all the piping and decorative seams are exactly tone-in-tone. All cockpit trim elements boast a finish in BRABUS “Shadow Gray”, setting further striking accents throughout the interior.

Like all BRABUS Masterpiece interior appointments, it is the precision with which all work was carried out that defines the "ALL GRAY" as well. This includes the quilting and perforation of the seat surfaces, center consoles and door panels in "Seashell Diamond" pattern design with embossed "Double-B” logos. The floors of the vehicle interior and the trunk are made from the finest leather and are adorned by the same pattern. The seat side bolsters as well as the inner sections of the door panels commemorate the founding of BRABUS in 1977 with the “Heritage” brand pattern featuring embossed “77” logos.

A selection of high-gloss sealed BRABUS Signature Carbon elements integrated into the dashboard, steering wheel, center console and door panels set further sporty accents inside the cabin. The interior is rounded off by a selection of additional custom features, such as the BRABUS carbon pedals and the BRABUS aluminum entry panels complete with an illuminated BRABUS logo, which changes color in tune with the ambient lighting.
