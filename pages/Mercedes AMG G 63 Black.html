THE ICON RETURNS. BRABUS 800 SUPERBLACK.

One thing is certain. Black will always equal BRABUS. With 800 horsepower under its unmistakable power dome hood and
a striking, all-black flair, the BRABUS 800 "SUPERBLACK" redefines the BRABUS look. It doesn’t just follow in the footsteps of its
predecessor—it blazes its own trail. Completely reimagined inside and out and based on the W 465 series Mercedes-AMG
G 63 platform, it is an evolution of cool monochrome luxury.

Interior. Superblack.

Finally, the BRABUS 800 "SUPERBLACK" also includes an exclusive, naturally black-on-black BRABUS Masterpiece interior that reflects the boundless precision the experts of the BRABUS interior workshop are renowned for in every single detail.

Featuring precise quilting of the seat surfaces, interior door trim parts and the floors of the interior and luggage compartments in the BRABUS Seashell Diamond design, the entire cabin matches the 1-Second-Wow effect of the exterior. Embossed BRABUS "Double-B" logos set further intricate details. The black seat surfaces are perforated with pinpoint accuracy and feature matching black piping as well as decorative stitching. The sides of each seat and selected parts of the seat surfaces, the sun visors and sections of the Masterpiece headliner, all likewise upholstered in the finest leather, are accentuated by the exclusive BRABUS logo pattern.
A selection of interior carbon elements strikes a perfect balance between elegance and sportiness. From the BRABUS pedal pads and ergonomic paddle shifters to sections of the steering wheel, dashboard trim, door panels and center console, every detail is meticulously crafted to impress. Carbon fiber entry panels on all four doors add an additional touch of exclusivity, featuring a backlit BRABUS logo that changes color in sync with the ambient lighting. The cockpit controls, air vents, loudspeaker grilles and various trim elements are finished in BRABUS “Shadow Gray,” offering a sleek and refined contrast to the all-black interior.
