Live to Stand Out. BRABUS 600 PEETCH.

A bold new flavor of luxury and a fresh new look for an old favorite. The BRABUS 900 "PEETCH" is a cheeky twist on
BRABUS high performance, blending 900 horsepower with an undeniable 1-Second-Wow aesthetic that’s simply
impossible to ignore. From the bespoke carbon fiber bodywork to the high-end Monoblock wheels, all the way to
the exclusive Masterpiece interior design, every inch of this striking supercar is finished in a playful, custom peach
hue.

Interior. Peetch.

The cockpit of the BRABUS 900 "PEETCH" is a world of bespoke luxury. Every single detail echoes the unmistakable 1-Second-Wow effect of its exterior. Our specialists created a fully customized, hand-finished BRABUS Masterpiece leather design, impressing with the same vibrant BRABUS “Peetch” aesthetic. Extravagant BRABUS Seashell Diamond quilting adorns the seat surfaces, door panels and floors of both the cockpit and the luggage compartment. The tone-in-tone look is accentuated by matching stitching and piping. The leather on the seat surfaces and door panels was also precisely perforated, adding a refined touch to the interior's breathable luxury.

The interplay between the rich leather and carbon elements across the center console, steering wheel and dashboard highlights the fusion of luxury and performance. In keeping with the BRABUS DNA, these high-performance carbon fiber details are complemented by sleek aluminum components like the BRABUS pedals and a footrest with backlit BRABUS logo. Further enhancing the cockpit’s aesthetic are the interior trim elements, finished in BRABUS "Shadow Gray," providing a subtle contrast to the striking “Peetch” leather, as well as carbon entry panels with illuminated BRABUS logo.
