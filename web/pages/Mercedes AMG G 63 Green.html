THE ICON RETURNS. BRABUS 800 MEAN GREEN.

The BRABUS 800 "MEAN GREEN" is a bespoke Masterpiece of the Collection 2025 that once again showcases the power of color at its absolu-
te best. Every curve, every detail stands out against its monochrome, green signature hue. There’s no distraction, no unnecessary noise—just
pure, unadulterated style. From its aggressively designed WIDESTAR bodywork to its Masterpiece interior, every detail was meticulously devel-
oped to exude confidence. The supercar is based on the current W 465 series Mercedes-AMG G 63.

Interior. Mean Green.

The BRABUS 800 "MEAN GREEN" features an exclusive, hand-crafted BRABUS Masterpiece interior that perfectly mirrors the bold, monochrome character of its exterior. With BRABUS quilting applied throughout the seat surfaces, door panels and floors of both the cabin and the luggage compartment in the Seashell Diamond design, the interior delivers an immediate wow effect. The luxurious opal leather upholstery, accented with piping and stitching in the same unique tone, adds a twist to the base aesthetic. The seats are not only finely quilted but also feature precision perforation in combination with embossed “Double-B” logos enhancing the signature styling.

Further touches include the BRABUS logo pattern on the side seat bolsters, the sun visors and select areas of the Masterpiece headliner, all likewise hand-crafted from the finest opal-colored leather. High-grade Alcantara in emerald green blends with the leather throughout the cabin, creating an elegant material contrast.

Sporty carbon accents add a high-performance edge, impressing with the same green hue as the exclusive WIDESTAR exterior components. Carbon fiber paddle shifters and pedals, elements of the steering wheel as well as trims on the dashboard, door panels and center console, create a seamless fusion of luxury and performance styling. The carbon entry panels for all four doors welcome passengers with an illuminated BRABUS logo that changes color in sync with the ambient lighting system. The controls, air vents, loudspeaker grilles and various trim elements of the cockpit are finished in BRABUS “Shadow Gray,” adding an extra layer of brand-typical Masterpiece luxury.
