ELEGANT. BRABUS 900 DEEP BLUE.

Like the carefully laid threads of a fitted blue suit, the BRABUS 900 DEEP BLUE is a staple of any classic wardrobe. Impressing with
its 900 hp performance output and its unmistakable “Deep Blue” exterior, it seamlessly blends power with precision and, in doing
so, takes the concept of bold elegance to new heights. It is luxury reimagined for those who demand performance without comp-
romising style. It is captivating design with a typical BRABUS twist.

Interior. Light Blue

Inside, the BRABUS 900 DEEP BLUE offers an ocean of bespoke luxury developed to tastefully complement the supercar’s bold exterior with an equally extravagant 1-Second-Wow effect. Featuring the finest BRABUS “Ice Blue” leather throughout, the entire cockpit is adorned with exquisite BRABUS “Ellipse” quilting on the seat surfaces, door panels and floors of both the cockpit and the luggage compartment. The elegant, tone-in-tone design is enhanced by matching piping and stitching in the same vibrant hue, creating a harmonious and refined aesthetic. The seat surfaces and door panels are also precisely perforated, offering an elegant and breathable touch to the luxurious atmosphere.

Carbon elements across the dashboard, center console and steering wheel reflect the brand-typical combination of performance and style. The interior trim elements, finished in BRABUS “Shadow Gray,” provide a subtle yet striking contrast. Aluminum pedals and a footrest with backlit BRABUS logo integrate seamlessly into the design while offering optimal grip. Carbon entry panels, likewise featuring a backlit BRABUS logo, complete the look, adding a final touch of exclusivity to the BRABUS 900 DEEP BLUE’s interior.
