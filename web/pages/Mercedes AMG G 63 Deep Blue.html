THE ICON RETURNS. BRABUS 800 DEEP BLUE.

This is the BRABUS 800 "DEEP BLUE". No compromise is needed where this daily-driver Masterpiece combines cool “Deep Blue”
aesthetics with high-end performance that packs a knock-out punch—enhanced in its design by rich Masterpiece interior te-
xtures and a unique overall luxury feel achieved through advanced individualization techniques. As the third exclusive launch
variant of the new BRABUS 800 product family based on the current W 465 series Mercedes-AMG G 63, it offers a different pe-
rspective on the details of its typical monochrome BRABUS styling and meticulously refined WIDESTAR bodywork concept.

Interior. Deep Blue.

Inside, the BRABUS 800 "DEEP BLUE" features an exclusive BRABUS Masterpiece interior. Its signature quilting in the BRABUS “Double Cube” pattern covers the seat surfaces, door panels and floors of both the cabin and luggage compartment. The luxurious slate gray leather upholstery, accentuated by blue piping and stitching, adds a vivid contrast that echoes the bold exterior style. Precision perforations and embossed “Double-B” logos on the seat surfaces further underscore the exclusivity of the design.

Additional accents include the BRABUS logo pattern adorning the side seat bolsters, select seat surfaces, sun visors and parts of the Masterpiece headliner, all meticulously hand-crafted from the finest slate gray leather. High-grade Alcantara sections blend harmoniously with the leather throughout the cabin, providing an elegant material contrast.

Stylish BRABUS Signature Carbon elements stand out with a matching blue hue. From the carbon fiber paddle shifters and pedals, elements of the steering wheel as well as the trims on the dashboard, door panels and center console, the high-grade compound seamlessly merges luxury with performance. Carbon entry panels on all four doors feature backlit BRABUS logos that shift in color to match the ambient interior lighting. True to the series, the controls, air vents, speaker grilles and various trim elements likewise boast a striking blue finish, adding a final, bespoke design detail.
